DONATE CRYPTOCURRENCY (recommended)

Even if the cryptocurrencies aren't used widely, yet, and the uasage is somewhat complicated, there are some important advantages over usual forms of payment. There are at least two reasons that we prefer to use them on our website:

1) fees: sending the cryptocurrency from one public address to another is very cheap (the public addresses are similar to the bank accounts, except there are no first names or surnames being used, so their owners remain anonymous).
2) transparency: everyone can verify the balance or transactions of any public address.

For our project we've chosen the BUSD cryptocurrency, because this is one of the very few "stable coins", so it never loses its value: one BUSD is always equal to one american dollar.
